Privacy Policy for Rádio Moçambique

Effective date: April 4, 2026

Rádio Moçambique ("we", "our", or "the app") is a radio streaming application. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and shared when you use the app.

1. Information Stored on Your Device

The app stores certain information locally on your device to support app features and improve your experience. This may include favorite stations, recently played stations, and app settings such as theme, language, and stream preferences. This information is stored locally on your device and is not stored on our own servers.

2. Radio Streaming and Station Interactions

The app connects to online radio streams and station directory services. When you play a station, the app may send requests to third-party services such as station directories or radio stream providers. These requests may include technical information needed to deliver audio and support station availability.

3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

Rádio Moçambique may use third-party services such as Google AdMob to display advertisements. These third-party services may collect, process, and share certain information, including advertising identifiers, device or other identifiers, IP address, app interaction data related to ads, and general analytics or ad performance data.

These services may use this information for ad delivery, ad measurement, analytics, fraud prevention, and service improvement. Google and its partners may also use IP-based signals to infer general location for advertising purposes. The app does not request precise GPS location.

You can learn more about Google’s advertising practices here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Data Security

We do not operate user accounts or store personal profiles on our own servers. However, the app communicates with third-party radio streams and services, and not all connections may be encrypted.

5. Information We Do Not Directly Collect

We do not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location. The app does not require account creation or login.

6. Children’s Privacy

Rádio Moçambique is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

7. Your Choices

You may clear the app’s local data through your device settings, reset or delete your advertising ID through Android settings, or uninstall the app at any time.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected in the latest version made available for the app.
